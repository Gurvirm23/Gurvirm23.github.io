Refund Policy

Overview

We strive to ensure you are satisfied with your purchase. If you are not completely satisfied, our refund policy provides detailed instructions on how to request a refund.

Refund Eligibility

Items must be returned in their original condition within 30 days of purchase. The following items are non-refundable:

  • Gift cards
  • Personalized or custom items
  • Items marked as non-returnable

Requesting a Refund

To initiate a refund, contact our support team with your order number and reason for return:

  • Email: support@ezsnkrs.com
  • Phone: 1-800-123-4567

After receiving and inspecting the returned item, we will process your refund within 5-7 business days.

Late or Missing Refunds

If you haven’t received a refund yet, first check your bank account again. Then contact your credit card company, as it may take some time before your refund is officially posted.

Exchanges

We only replace items if they are defective or damaged. If you need to exchange it for the same item, contact our support team.

Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are non-refundable. If you receive a refund, the cost of return shipping will be deducted from your refund.
