Legit Check: Trust The Process

Our global team of experts uses a rigorous, multi-step verification process to ensure every product is authentic and meets our high standards.

Our Verification Process

  • Condition: We only allow deadstock on our marketplace. That means every item must be brand new and never worn.
  • Packaging: Equally important as the product itself, our team ensures packaging meets the highest quality standards to deliver a brand new product.
  • Accessories: From the full list of accessories to all the additional add-ons, rest assured that your purchase will match any retail purchase experience.

Advanced Technology

  • Experience & Data: We've inspected over 35 million items on behalf of our customers.
  • Advanced Technology: We leverage machine learning and embedded technologies within the products to enhance our verification efforts.
  • Quality Assurance: A final check by our QA experts ensures the product meets our high standards.
